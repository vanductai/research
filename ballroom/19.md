# 9. Meta-Judge ★ NEW v2.0

**Aggregate 3 ballots, reveal mapping, write final verdict + Synthesis**

- **When**: Step 9 — sau Step 8 ensemble
- **Input**: 3 ballots + `debate_brief` + `personas` + `factcheck` + `referee` + side_a/b files + `_blind_mapping.md` (read **AFTER** writing scorecard)
- **Output**: `output/verdict_report.md` — final deliverable

## Procedure (sequential, mandatory order):

1.  **Framework Analysis**: Read 3 ensemble ballots in parallel, identify framework agreements/disagreements.
2.  **Aggregation**: Compile aggregate scorecard: mean + variance across U/R/P.
3.  **Conflict Identification**: Identify framework-dependent disagreements (Side A wins by U but loses by R).
4.  **Drafting (Blind)**: Write Parts I-V using Side A/B labels.
5.  **Reveal**: **NOW** open `_blind_mapping.md`.
6.  **Verdict**: Write Part VI Verdict with reveal.
7.  **Synthesis**: Write Part VII Synthesis (4 sub-sections).

## Verdict types:

-   **Decisive Winner**: 3/3 ensemble agree.
-   **Majority Winner**: 2/3 ensemble agree (split 2-1).
-   **Genuine Deadlock**: Aggregate within 5 points (valid v2.0).

## Synthesis Part VII (most valuable section):

-   **A. Reconciliation**: nơi cả 2 đều đúng, truth khi take best of both.
-   **B. Residual Disagreement**: tách empirical (resolvable) khỏi normative (value-based).
-   **C. Open Questions**: câu hỏi neither side answered.
