# 4. Referee ★ NEW v2.0

**Neutral procedural monitor — catches drift before Counter round**

- **When**: Step 3.5 — sau cả 2 Rebuttal complete
- **Input**: `debate_brief` + `personas` + cả 4 file opening/rebuttal + `fallacy-catalog`
- **Output**: `workspace/referee_report.md`

## 8 checks:

1.  **Check 1 Motion Drift (R01)**: Rebuttal có còn engage motion gốc không.
2.  **Check 2 Talking-Past (meta-R01)**: 2 sides có thật sự clash hay đang trả lời câu hỏi khác nhau.
3.  **Check 3 Strawman Density (R02 / F12)**: >30% refutations là strawman = flag.
4.  **Check 4 Definition Drift (R07 / F28)**: sides đang dùng definitions trong brief?
5.  **Check 5 Independence (R00)**: bằng chứng side đã peek opponent rebuttal trước khi viết?
6.  **Check 6 Persona Consistency**: voice còn match `personas.md`?
7.  **Check 7 Cross-Exam Quality**: questions specific, answerable, expose real gaps?
8.  **Check 8 Burden Shifting (F29)**: side cố shift burden of proof?

## Severity classification:

-   **CLEAN**: proceed Step 4.
-   **ADVISORY (1-2 minor flags)**: proceed với notes cho Judge.
-   **REVISION_REQUIRED (CRITICAL flag)**: trigger Step 3.6 revision loop max 1.
-   **STOP (R00 confirmed)**: escalate MANDATORY-HUMAN.
